Comprehensive Review
In Seven Dimensions

Every paper is evaluated across seven critical academic criteria, ensuring thorough and balanced feedback.

Clarity & Writing Quality

Evaluates readability, grammar, coherence, and overall writing quality of your paper.

Novelty / Originality

Assesses how unique and innovative your research contribution is compared to existing work.

Technical Soundness

Checks the validity of claims, correctness of reasoning, and soundness of conclusions.

Methodology & Rigor

Reviews the research design, experimental setup, and rigor of the methods used.

Significance / Impact

Measures the potential impact and importance of the research to the broader field.

Structure & Organization

Analyzes the logical flow, section arrangement, and overall organization of the paper.

Literature & References

Evaluates the quality, relevance, and comprehensiveness of cited references.
